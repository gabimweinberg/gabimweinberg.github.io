Hey, I'm Gabi Weinberg, and I call myself the Startup Rabbi because I believes that startup skills and entrepreneurial spirit need to be infused into Jewish organizations, institutions, and the community at-large.

I have spent a number of years working with teens and among my peers to create programs and events that involve different members of the community. These activities come from my passion for connecting people and executing ideas. I hopes to synergize my study of the ancient texts with the modern modalities of community building and startup methodology. I ultimately hope to catapult the Jewish people into being a more 21st century conscious community.

Check out upcoming events that I'm running. They will start in the New York area, but we will hopefully expand nationwide as time goes on.
